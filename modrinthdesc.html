Heru’s Vanilla+

Want to play modded Minecraft? Don’t wanna play a new game with how some Mod packs are? Want to play ***Vanilla but Plus?*** WARNING: This pack is for friends group and SMP. The WHOLE pack CAN AND WILL change at any time. If you like your version you are always welcome to use the older versions of the pack. --- Currently, I am testing this Pack out for that Vanilla Feel but with a bit more spice! Make your home feel like a real home with new items and blocks that keep the vanilla feel! ---
